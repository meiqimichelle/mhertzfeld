About This Site

Behind the curtain My site is powered by Jekyll, a Ruby-based site generator that uses Liquid as its templating language. It is hosted entirely on GitHub (thanks to Kin Lane and his Hacker Storytelling for the technique!).

On stage My design was built on top of Skeleton, a bare-bones responsive framework. Fonts are Bree from Type Together, and Freight Sans from Darden Studio.
